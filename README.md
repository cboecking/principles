# Principles

The purpose of this repository is to describe the principles I wish to pursue now and for the rest of my life.

# What Matters

- Surround myself with people that are worthy of my love and trust
- Surround myself with people who inspire me
- Surround myself with people who are:
  - kind
  - curious
  - patient with people
  - impatient with progress
  - balanced
  - reflective
  - open minded
  - courageous 
- Generate wealth and opportunity for everyone I engage with
